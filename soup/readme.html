HOW DOES THIS WORK?

Welcome to PancakeDragoness's Soup Message Generator! To construct a message, you'll need to choose each of its parts. A basic message has two parts, the template and the fill phrase.

An example template looks like this: "****!"

While an example fill phrase is a word like "soup" or "computer".

Because there are many fill phrases to choose from, they are further organized into categories. To access them, simply choose the category you want from the category dropdown and a new dropdown will appear with the fill phrases from that category. For example, "soup" and "computer" are both in "Things".

COPYING YOUR MESSAGE

Copying your message to the clipboard for using elsewhere is super simple. Just click the copy to clipboard button, and it'll be copied automatically for you to paste wherever you like! Please use soup messages responsibly!

ADVANCED MODES

There are more advanced templates to choose from, too! Activating complex mode by checking its box will give you three new dropdowns to use:

Two are a new pair of template & fill phrase dropdowns, allowing you to construct a second message just like the first.

But to finalize your complex message, you'll also need to choose a conjunction from the center dropdown. This phrase joins the two messages you've made to create one big message!

An example conjunction is a word like "and" or "but".

TWO-PHRASE TEMPLATE MODE

There's also two-phrase template mode! In this mode, there are no conjunctions, but you can still choose two different fill phrases. You get a whole new set of templates to work with that combine the two phrases you choose in non-standard ways.

An example two-phrase template looks like this: "This **** is ****, isn't it?"

Please enjoy these advanced modes! I look forward to seeing what messages you can create using this tool!
